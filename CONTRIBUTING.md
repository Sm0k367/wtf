🚀 How to Contribute to EPIC TECH AI

Every commit should feel like a level-up.
Only 🔥 bangers get merged—bring your best, push the limits.
Anything boring, generic, or mid? Cut it.
PRs: 1 feature/fix/change at a time. Explain what makes yours worth shipping.
Keep code modular, readable, and leverage the best AI/model for the job.
No secrets (.env, API keys) in PRs—set those in Vercel or your own .env.
Getting Started
Fork the repo, clone your fork, and create a branch:
git checkout -b feature/shiny-new-thing
Make your changes/innovation.
Push to your fork/branch.
Open a PR with a punchy description.
Wait for review & a little roasting by SmokeStream.
We build wild stuff here and take pride in it. "Safe" and "predictable" live somewhere else.
