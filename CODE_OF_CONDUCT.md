CODE OF CONDUCT
Welcome to the Jungle
Respect all contributors. We bash ideas, not people.
Challenge everything, but do it with style and good vibes.
No hate, no harassment, no creepy DMs, no gatekeeping—ever.
Don’t leak private info, keys, or secrets.
If your “feedback” isn’t constructive, don’t bother.
Accept being roasted—especially if it makes the project better.
Reporting
If someone’s out of bounds, ping Sm0k367 or open an issue with [conduct] in the title.
